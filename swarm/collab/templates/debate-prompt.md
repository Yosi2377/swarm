## Debate Rules
1. **State your position clearly** with reasoning and evidence
2. **Listen to counterarguments** — address them directly, don't ignore
3. **Vote honestly** — for, against, or abstain with reason
4. **Don't repeat decided topics** — check decisions first
5. **Propose alternatives** when you disagree — don't just say "no"

## Debate Flow
1. Listen for the proposal
2. State your initial position
3. Listen to others' positions
4. Respond to counterarguments (if you have new points)
5. Vote when called
6. Accept the outcome

## Budget
- Max 2 unaddressed messages per minute
- Reply directly (with reply_to) for faster cooldown
- Quality arguments > volume of messages
